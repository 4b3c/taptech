Custom keychain merch with embedded NFC chips—tap to open your club’s website, socials, or an event page.

Made locally for student organizations and small businesses. Logo customization + NFC programming included.

How it works

Each keychain contains a small NFC chip programmed with a link you choose. When someone taps the keychain with a smartphone, their phone automatically opens your URL—no QR code, no typing.

1) You send

Your logo + the link you want (Instagram, Linktree, Discord invite, website, event page, etc.).

2) I build

I customize the design and program the NFC chips. I’ll confirm the look and the link before producing the batch.

3) You use

Hand them out at tabling, meetings, or events. People tap and instantly land where you want them.

NFC (Near Field Communication) is built into most modern iPhones and Android phones, so tapping works for the majority of people.
